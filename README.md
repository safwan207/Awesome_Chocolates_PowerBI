Project OverView
Awesome Chocolates is a dynamic sales analytics project designed to track global performance and profitability. Using Power BI, I transformed raw data into interactive insights, featuring a 60.3% profit margin and detailed shipment analysis. It highlights key trends across regions like India and Canada using advanced DAX and Power Query.

Key Metrics & Analysis
Sales & Profitability: Analyzed total sales revenue and profit margins, identifying the most profitable chocolate products and underperforming categories.
Shipment & Logistics: Tracked shipment volumes and "LBs" (pounds) of product moved to identify supply chain patterns and peak distribution periods.
Salesperson Benchmarking: Created a "People" leaderboard to evaluate individual sales team performance based on targets and total revenue generated.
Geographical Trends: Visualized sales data across different countries to pinpoint high-growth markets and regions requiring strategic focus.

Tools & Techniques Used
Power BI Desktop: Developed a multi-page interactive dashboard with custom themes.
Data Modeling: Established relationships between sales, products, geography, and team member tables.
DAX (Data Analysis Expressions): Created measures for year-to-date (YTD) sales, profit calculations, and performance percentages.
Visual Storytelling: Utilized custom icons (costs, profits, shipments) and consistent branding to make the data accessible for executive decision-making.
